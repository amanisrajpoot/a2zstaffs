# A2Z Staffing Solutions - Modern Website Plan

## Design Philosophy
- **Modern & Beautiful**: Clean, professional design with smooth animations
- **Feature-Rich**: All essential staffing business features
- **Simple Code**: Maintainable, consistent structure
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first, works on all devices
- **Dark Mode**: Full dark mode support

## Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Professional Blue (#2563eb)
- **Secondary**: Sky Blue (#0ea5e9)
- **Accent**: Success Green, Warning Orange, Error Red
- **Neutrals**: Clean grays with proper contrast
- **Gradients**: Subtle gradients for depth

## Typography
- **Headings**: Bold, modern sans-serif
- **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- **Sizes**: Clear hierarchy (h1: 3.5rem, h2: 2.5rem, etc.)

## Pages Structure

### 1. Homepage (/)
- **Hero Section**: Large, impactful hero with CTA buttons
- **Services Overview**: 4-6 service cards with icons
- **Stats Section**: Key numbers (candidates placed, clients served, etc.)
- **How It Works**: Step-by-step process for both candidates and clients
- **Testimonials**: Client and candidate testimonials
- **CTA Section**: Final call-to-action

### 2. About Us (/about)
- **Hero**: Company story introduction
- **Mission & Vision**: Clear mission and vision statements
- **Values**: Core company values
- **Team**: Team members (optional, can be placeholder)
- **History**: Company timeline/milestones

### 3. Services (/services)
- **Overview**: All services listed
- **Service Cards**: Detailed service descriptions
- **Industries Served**: Industries we work with
- **Process**: How we work

### 4. Jobs (/jobs)
- **Search & Filters**: Search bar and filter options
- **Job Listings**: Grid/list view of open positions
- **Job Cards**: Title, location, type, salary range, description
- **Apply CTA**: Direct apply button on each job

### 5. For Candidates (/candidates)
- **Hero**: Why choose us as a candidate
- **Benefits**: Benefits of working with us
- **Process**: How to apply
- **Application Form**: Quick application form
- **Success Stories**: Candidate success stories

### 6. For Clients (/clients)
- **Hero**: Why choose us as a client
- **Services**: Detailed client services
- **Process**: How we work with clients
- **Request Talent Form**: Form to request talent
- **Client Testimonials**: Client testimonials

### 7. Contact (/contact)
- **Contact Info**: Phone, email, address
- **Contact Form**: Full contact form
- **Map**: Location map (optional)
- **Office Hours**: Business hours

## Components Needed

### Reusable Components
1. **Hero**: Large hero sections with title, subtitle, CTA
2. **ServiceCard**: Service display cards
3. **JobCard**: Job listing cards
4. **TestimonialCard**: Testimonial display
5. **StatCard**: Statistics display
6. **Button**: Consistent button styles
7. **FormInput**: Form input fields
8. **Section**: Section wrapper with consistent spacing

## Features

### Forms
- **Job Application Form**: Name, email, phone, resume upload, cover letter
- **Talent Request Form**: Company info, requirements, timeline
- **Contact Form**: Name, email, subject, message
- **All forms**: Netlify Forms integration ready

### Interactive Elements
- **Smooth Scroll**: Smooth scrolling navigation
- **Hover Effects**: Subtle hover animations
- **Loading States**: Form submission states
- **Theme Toggle**: Dark/light mode toggle
